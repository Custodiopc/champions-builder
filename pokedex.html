About the Pokémon Champions Roster

Pokémon Champions features a curated roster of 204 Pokémon selected for competitive VGC doubles battles. The roster includes fan favorites across all generations, multiple regional forms (Alolan, Hisuian, Galarian), and a wide selection of Mega Evolutions. Each Pokémon page on Champions Builder provides the complete legal moveset in Pokémon Champions, base stats, available abilities, and a recommended Stat Point (SP) spread to help you build competitively.
